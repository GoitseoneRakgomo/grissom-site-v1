Frequently Asked Questions

Grissom is a corporate services company that provides administrative, governance, recruitment, and strategic support to businesses operating across multiple jurisdictions. It serves as a centralised support function, helping ensure that companies remain well-structured, efficiently managed, and appropriately supported over the long term.

Grissom supports only selected companies. Contact us if you are interested in working with us.

Grissom coordinates a range of internal corporate services, including corporate administration, governance coordination, recruitment and talent acquisition, organisational support, internal operational assistance, strategic coordination, documentation and record management, and administrative oversight. These services help maintain consistency, efficiency, and continuity.

Grissom acts as the centralised recruitment function for companies within the selected network of companies and business partners with which it maintains ongoing administrative and support relationships. As a result, job advertisements, candidate communications, interview coordination, and onboarding processes may be managed by Grissom even when the position itself is with another company.

Grissom acts solely as a recruitment coordinator and administrative facilitator. Final hiring decisions are made by the relevant employing entity.

Employment arrangements vary depending on the position and location. While recruitment may be coordinated through Grissom, successful candidates may ultimately be employed by the relevant company for which the role exists.

Details regarding employment arrangements will be provided during the recruitment process.
